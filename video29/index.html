BMW M5

The BMW M5 is a high-performance sports sedan known for its powerful engine, dynamic handling, and luxurious features. It seamlessly blends the practicality of a business sedan with the thrills of a high-performance sports car. The latest M5, in particular, features a hybrid powertrain combining a powerful M TwinPower Turbo V8 with an electric motor, delivering a system output of 727 horsepower.
